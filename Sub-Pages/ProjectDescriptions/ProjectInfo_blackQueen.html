The Black Queen (2019)

The Black Queen is an interactive virtual actor made for live theatre. The virtual actor reacts to various modes of interaction such as hand gestures and motion tracking. The movement of the virtual actor is based on the flocking algorithm, and responds to both actions made by the actress and predefined ques.

Software: Unity, Open Frameworks.
Hardware: Kinect, two laptops, three projectors.
Materials: paint and acrylic screens

Collaborators: Louise Lessel, Amalie Solander, Sophus Benée Olsen.
The virtual actor was part of the multi media opera Queen Barbora: The Black Queen, directed by Juraj Kojs.

Performances
MDC Live Arts Lab, September 27th & 28th, 2018 Miami, Florida
Queens Festival, May 14th & 15th, 2019, Copenhagen, Denmark
