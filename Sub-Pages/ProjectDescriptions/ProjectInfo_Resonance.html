Resonance (2022)

A generative virtual reality experience which explores the urge for resonating with other worlds, beings and mediums. Untouched, the experience can be a remedy or refuge where humans and artificial lifeforms fluently can co-exist and interconnect.

Length: 5 minutes
Software: Standalone Quest Application, built with Unity
Hardware: Quest 2, headphones

Exhibited: Die Angewandte Festival

The piece was developed as a computational exploration of the life cycle of moon jellies, where the audience is participating as the streams of the ocean. In this otherworldly experience, the audience aid the lively abstractions of jellyfish in the release from their strobila. The performative aspect of the experience invites the audience to act in a dance between the human and the simulated. Music for the experience is an old recording by Nina-Franziska herself.
