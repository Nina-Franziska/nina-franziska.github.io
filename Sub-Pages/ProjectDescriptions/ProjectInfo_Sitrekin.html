Sitrekin Interactive Concert Visuals (2024 - )

Audio and movement reactive concert visuals for Sitrekin, the new artist project by renowned musician Kirstine Stubbe Teglbjærg (former lead singer in Blue Foundation and co-founder of Hun Solo).
The collaboration is ongoing, and will be continued at the release of the forthcoming album.

Software and algorithms: Touchdesigner, Blender, generative AI, OSC.
Hardware: Kinect Azure, Laptop, router, projector, sound card.
Collaborators: Sitrekin (Kirstine Stubbe Teglbjærg) and Uberørt (Louise Lessél and Nina-Franziska Christiansen).

Shows
Spot Festival, May 3rd, 2024, Aarhus, Denmark.
Lille Vega, February 2nd, 2024, Copenhagen, Denmark.


Together with Kirstine, we developed a visual language for Sitrekin based on our common visual references and the thematics slow growth, organic movement, alchemy, and divine female strength. The concerts visuals speaks to the complexity of her being, while building upon the narrative and emotion within each of her songs.
Kirstine Stubbe Teglbjærg is the former lead singer of Blue Foundation (known for their single Eyes On Fire, featured on the Twilight Soundtrack) and co-founded the feminist music movement Hun Solo. She is known for her etheral voice and vivid songwriting, and stands out as a distinctive contributor to the success of Blue Foundation. She has been featured in Rolling Stones, and is supported by the Danish Arts Foundation.
