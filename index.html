NINA-FRANZISKA
About Uberørt

Visual artist, designer
and Creative Technologist

creating immersive real-time experiences
across the physical and virtual space

Expanding the boundaries of performative technology through code and design of interactive immersive experiences.
From neuro science to extended realities, and with collaborators such as SØSTR (Sharin Foo, Louise Foo) and the Royal Danish Academy, Nina-Franziska moves between the synthetic, the abstract, and the organic.
While encompassing the audience in sound and vision, she guides them through the otherworldly - questioning what is real, as the physical and virtual becomes one.

Nina-Franziska is half of the creative duo Uberørt.
